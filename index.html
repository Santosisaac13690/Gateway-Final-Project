Information

Located in the heart of Central America, Honduras blows away all tourists with its natural beauty, diverse wildlife & rich culture. From astonishing coral reefs to mouth watering food Honduras has it all. Nature lovers will have a blast visiting the beautiful clear water beaches overflowing with coral reefs & underwater wildlife. History buffs will enjoy learning about our rich culture at the stunning museums & visiting the ancient mayan ruins. Foodies will definitely gain a few pounds coming to Honduras because the food is immaculate. Make sure to try pupusas, baleadas & honduran horchata.

Why I want to visit

My father is from El Salvador & my mother is from Honduras. The two countries neighbor each other & i've always wanted to visit my parents home countries. When I was 10 years old I had the pleasure of visiting El Salvador with my father but I haven't had the chance to visit Honduras with my mother. Although both my parents are undocumented my father had a travel permit at the time so he took me & my sisters to visit his home country. Unfortunately my mother didnt get to go due to her legal status but my dream is to one day grant my parents citizenship & our family to visit Honduras.
